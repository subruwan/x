Sri Lanka improves in global corruption rankings

Last updated: Feb 10, 2026

Sri Lanka has improved its standing in Transparency International’s Global Corruption Index 2025, moving up from rank 121 to 107 out of 182 countries. The country’s score rose from 32 to 35, marking a +3 change since 2024, reflecting a slight improvement in perceptions of public sector integrity.
