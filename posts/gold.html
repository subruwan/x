Gold prices in Sri Lanka increase by Rs.12,000 per sovereign

Gold prices in Sri Lanka recorded a sharp increase on Tuesday [Feb 3, 2026], reversing the significant decline seen the previous day, in line with fluctuations in the global gold market, traders at Colombo Sea Street said.

The price of a 24-carat gold sovereign rose by Rs. 12,000 to Rs. 380,000, up from Rs. 368,000 on Monday [Feb 2, 2026]. Similarly, the price of a 22-carat gold sovereign increased to Rs. 349,000 from Rs. 338,000 a day earlier.
