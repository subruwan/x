TIN certificate mandatory for several services from April 1, 2026

Under the provisions of the Inland Revenue (Amendment) Act of 2026, a Taxpayer Identification Number (TIN) certificate will become a mandatory requirement for several key services & transactions in Sri Lanka starting April 1, 2026.

  • Open any account at any financial institution
  • Obtain approval for a building plan
  • Register a motor vehicle
  • Renew the licence of a motor vehicle
  • Register a land or title to a land
  • Register a business
  • Transfer shares of a company incorporated in Sri Lanka, by the transferee & transferor
  • Obtain a credit card
