CONVERT TEXT INTO AUDIO FOR FREE

The makers of PDFgear have released a free online tool that converts text into audio. Visit TextaVoice, type or paste the copy you want to hear (up to 2,000 characters) then choose a language (accent) & voice. Click ‘Advanced Settings’ to alter the speed & pitch, then Generate to hear it. You can download the result too.
